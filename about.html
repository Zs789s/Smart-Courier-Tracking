Smart Courier System (SCS) was founded in 2024 by a team of logistics and technology enthusiasts who recognized a gap in the delivery market. Traditional courier services lacked transparency and real-time tracking capabilities that modern customers demanded.

We set out to create a courier service that combines cutting-edge technology with reliable delivery operations. Today, SCS has grown to become one of the fastest-growing delivery startups, trusted by thousands of customers and businesses across the country.

Our mission is simple: Make delivery fast, transparent, and accessible to everyone.
